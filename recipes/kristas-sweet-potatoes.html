Krista's Sweet Potatoes

Sweet Potato Souffle

This is one of my family's favorite holiday dishes. Depending on how you like them the sweet potatoes can be very thick, or if preferred a bit runny. Either way the taste is phenomenal. Try it out this holiday season.

Ingredients

  • 2 large cans of sweet potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of orange juice
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • dash of nutmeg
  • dash of salt
  • 1 bag of mini marshmallows

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes until soft, drain well.
  2. Return to pot.
  3. Mix eggs and milk, then temper with a portion of sweet potato.
  4. Add tempered egg mixture and butter to pot, mash well.
  5. Add orange juice and dry ingredients. Mix well.
  6. If the mixture seems too wet, add flour as needed.
  7. Place mixture in pan and cook for minutes. Add marshmallows on top and cook until brown.
Note there is no specific temperature to cook in the oven.
