Cheesy Cowboy Casserole

This slow cooker meal has everything in it, from veggies to potatoes, meat and cheese. There are several ways to cook this dish, for example subbing in frozen potatoes and veggies is a variation. However we are not covering all of those here.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Mrs Dash
  • 1 lb red potatoes sliced thin
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup canned corn
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Brown and drain the ground beef
  2. Except for the cheese, place all ingredients in slow cooker and mix well.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 7-8hrs.
  4. Uncover and add shredded cheese across the top, then replace the lid.
  5. Cook for another 30min.
  6. Serve
