Biscuits


This recipe is an old school way of making biscuits at home. There are several ways of making them, but this is the base that I use. With a few tweaks here and there you can make them your own.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 8 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 5 tablespoons shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups milk or water + a bit more as needed.
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup sugar. Add to taste.

Steps

  1. Sift dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Blend shortening into flour.
  3. Make well in the center and pour the milk or water into it.
  4. Stir until flour is moistened.
  5. Toss on floured board or table, and knead for 20 seconds.
  6. Roll or pat dough until 1/2 - 3/4 inch thick, then cut into rounds.
  7. Place rounds onto a greased pan.
  8. Bake in 450â„‰ oven for 10-15 minutes.
